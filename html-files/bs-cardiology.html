BS CARDIOLOGY TECHNOLOGY

OVERVIEW

BS Cardiology Technology is a four-year Bachelor degree program that focuses on studying various diagnostic and therapeutic procedures used in cardiovascular system (CVS). These include congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, heart failure, valvular heart disease, and electrophysiology of the heart. Students will be able to apply their knowledge to analyze the CVS and will be able to utilize relevant computer assisted technologies.

The program equipe the students with the skills and knowledge required to operate, maintain and troubleshoot the advanced medical equipment used in cardiac care.

CAREER PROSPECTUS

Graduates of BS Cardiology Technology have a wide range of career options available to them. Some of the most popular career paths include:

Cardiac Technologist - These professionals work closely with consultant cardiologists to perform diagnostic tests and assist with various procedures like cardiac catheterization, angioplasty, and electrophysiology studies.

Cardiovascular Sonographer - These professionals use specialized ultrasound equipment to capture images of the heart and blood vessels, assisting in the diagnosis of various cardiac conditions.

Cardiac Rehabilitation Specialists - These professionals work with patients who have undergone cardiac surgery or treatment to help them recover and improve their cardiovascular health.

Medical Equipment Sales Representative - These professionals’ market and sell various medical equipment used in cardiac care, such as defibrillators, ECG machines, and pacemakers.
