About
the Group

At Punjab Group, we are grateful for the trust of millions of people who have placed their faith in us. We recognize that this trust comes with a great responsibility and we are committed to upholding it through our commitment to deliver the best.

As a leading force in academic excellence, mass media communication, and various business ventures in Pakistan, including renewable energy solutions, real estate, technology, and Agri-based products, we have been able to make a significant impact in uplifting the social fabric of our society.
