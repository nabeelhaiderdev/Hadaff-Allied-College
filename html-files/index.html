Introduction

hadaf college of allied health Sciences

Punjab Group of Colleges (PGC) is the largest educational network in Pakistan. It began in 1985 with a single campus in Lahore and has since become one of the country's top educational institutions. PGC now operates in 130 cities across Pakistan, with 451+ campuses. Hadaf Group of Colleges (A hallmark project of Punjab Group of Colleges) is well equipped with purpose-built campuses situated in city “Of Peshawar.” The purpose-built campuses provide excellent facilities and are staffed by highly qualified and dedicated faculty. The colleges offer comprehensive and challenging programs, accompanied by various activities, training programs, seminars, and workshops to help students explore and develop their talents.

Affiliation

Hadaf College of allied health sciences is affiliated with the most prestigious medical institute khyber medical university peshawar in the discipline of BS Anesthesia (4 year), BS Health (4 year), BS Radiology (4 year), BS Cardiology (4 year) and BS Surgical (4 year) in Semester System.
